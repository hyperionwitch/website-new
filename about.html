About the Artist
gillian renk - she/her
Gillian Renk is a shambling pile of goth dust and bird bones (read: artist) with a passion for historic art styles, digital painting, and comics. With a focus on fanart of video games and other media, she also dabbles in cosplay, photography, and web design. She finds the distance of third person description to be a comforting barrier to writing about herself, which is otherwise a completely miserable task for the Average Bisexual Millenial with imposter syndrome.
